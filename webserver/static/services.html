Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

What version of Go do you support?
We always support the latest stable version of Go, as well as the two previous major releases. You can specify your Go version in your project configuration.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing.
Do you offer a free trial for Pro?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for our Pro plan. No credit card required to start.
How does the auto-scaling work?
Our infrastructure monitors your application's CPU and memory usage. When thresholds are met, we automatically spin up new instances to handle the load, and scale down when traffic subsides.
